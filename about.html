About me

Hey, I'm delighted you're here.

I'm Adilberto "Wake" Torres IV — a junior at the University of Kansas studying Marketing. I grew up in Denver, Colorado, and these days I split my attention between coursework in Lawrence and the kind of marketing problems that keep me up overthinking late at night (in a good way).

Most of my early experience has lived at the intersection of research, strategy, and storytelling. At Next Generation HR in Missoula I learned how to turn raw industry data into something people actually want to read. At Casa Bonita in Denver — yes, that Casa Bonita — I learned how to lead a team during the kind of chaos only 2,500 daily guests can create. And right now, as a Marketing Consultant at Replay Lounge here in Lawrence, I'm helping rethink the brand's positioning from the ground up.

I like marketing because it's a puzzle with a personality. The fun part is figuring out who you're really talking to and why they should care — and then saying it in a way that doesn't feel like an ad.

What I'm looking for

I'm looking for summer 2026 internships in marketing, brand strategy, or market research — anywhere I can keep learning from people who do this well. If your team has a problem worth chewing on, I'd love to talk.

Outside of work

You can usually find me at a KU Investment Club meeting, playing roundnet (yes, Spikeball — I take it seriously), or trying to convince someone that Casa Bonita is a perfectly normal place to grow up around.
